### Post

Post API: `GET https://www.dcard.tw/api/post/all/<Post-ID>`

### Hot Post

Post API: `GET https://www.dcard.tw/api/forum/all/<Page-Number>/popular`

### Forum name: 全部

Forum API: `GET https://www.dcard.tw/api/forum/all/<Page-Number>`
